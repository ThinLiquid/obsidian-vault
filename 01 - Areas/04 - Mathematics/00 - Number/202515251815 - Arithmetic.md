## Mathematical symbols of equality and inequality
|  symbol | meaning                  |
| ------: | :----------------------- |
|     $=$ | equal to                 |
|   $\lt$ | less than                |
|   $\le$ | less than or equal to    |
|   $\gt$ | greater than             |
|   $\ge$ | greater than or equal to |
| $\not=$ | not equal to             |
